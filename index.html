Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is an underwater adventure that lets you explore the world below the surface. With gear and proper training, you can encounter marine life, dive to various depths, and create lifelong memories. Whether you're a beginner or experienced diver, scuba diving offers something for everyone in a safe and regulated environment.

About us.

Poseidon Diving Center is owned by George Pappas. George was born in Corfu in 1982 and from a very young age, he discovered his love for the sea and the underwater environment. George has many years of underwater experience and he is a certified Offshore Saturation diver IDSA level 4 with hundreds of dives in depths that are more than 200m. He is also a certified underwater welder and cutting with OXY-ARC, underwater constructor, ROV pilot, commercial divers instructor and IMCA DIVER MEDIC. In recreational diving, George is SSI and ANDI open water scuba instructor, diver medic instructor, night diving and low visibility instructor, dry suit instructor, diver medic instructor and nitrox instructor, cave diving instructor, wreck diving instructor, and deep diving instructor.
